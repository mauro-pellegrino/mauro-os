# 27-year-old hits $350K/month with hearing supplements for 60+

## Final post copy

A 27-year-old in our Skool community just crossed $350K/month and his product blew my mind.

Hearing supplements. For people over 60.

Not tinnitus. Not ear health. Specifically formulated for age-related hearing support.

He said he got the idea from his grandmother complaining about hearing loss. He went into the Meta Ad Library looking for hearing supplement brands and found almost nothing. A handful of brands with terrible creative and basic Shopify pages.

Wide open.

He built an advertorial-style funnel, filmed a VSL with a voice actor explaining the science, and launched with $2K in ad spend.

Within 60 days he was at $40K/month. By month 8 he hit $350K.

His audience is 55-75 year olds. His CPMs are low because almost nobody targets this age group with direct response supplement ads on Meta. His conversion rate is high because these people have a real problem and almost nobody is offering them a direct solution.

He's now expanding into Germany and France — two countries with aging populations and zero competition in this niche. Same funnel, translated and adapted to the European market with bundle-heavy offers instead of subscriptions.

The unsexy niches with older demographics are wildly underserved. Most ecom founders optimize for the 25-40 year old audience because that's who they see on their Instagram feed. The 55+ audience has more money, less price sensitivity, and almost nobody marketing to them properly on Meta.

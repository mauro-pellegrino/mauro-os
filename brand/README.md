# Mauro's Content Pipeline

## What this is
Raw session notes captured every time Mauro does something worth teaching — for his personal brand targeting agency owners who want to use AI in their business.

**Target audience:** B2B marketing agency owners who want to use AI to run better operations, create more content, and grow faster.

**Format:** One folder per session, named `YYYY-MM-DD-topic`. Inside each: a `raw-notes.md` file that captures everything — what was done, why, what problems came up, what decisions were made, what the thinking was at each step.

## What counts as a session
Everything. Not just AI work. Any time Mauro:
- Builds or sets up something for the agency
- Makes a strategic decision worth explaining
- Solves a problem another agency owner would face
- Tries something, fails, and figures out why
- Has a conversation that produces a useful insight

## How to use these notes
Raw notes are source material — not content. When there are enough sessions, patterns emerge and content (X articles, LinkedIn posts, YouTube scripts) gets written from them using the skills in `skills/content/`.

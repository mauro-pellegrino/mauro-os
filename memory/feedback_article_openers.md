Do not open every article with the same credential badge. Mauro flagged on 2026-06-04 that when nearly every article leads with the same credibility line it becomes a tic that trains the reader to skip the first 4 lines. The opener should be decided by the *input*: read the source material, find the single strongest, most specific hook that piece gives you, and lead with that. Options include a source/news hook (the thread or post the piece is built on), a contradiction/counterintuitive claim, an operational stat, or the credential anchor. Credibility does not have to live at the top; it often lands harder lower, attached to a specific claim (a specific result, e.g. [CLIENT RESULT: fill from real wins when available], usually beats a generic credential badge in line 1).

**Why:** Repetition of the same opener kills the hook and signals templated/AI content; the spend number stops earning attention when it shows up every time.

**How to apply:** When producing articles or posts with the x-article-creator skill (skills/content/x-article-creator.md), pick the opener type that fits the input; the skill was updated to make the opener input-driven.

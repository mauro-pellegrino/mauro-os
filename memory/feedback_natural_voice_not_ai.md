For normal posts (not auto-DMs), write the way a real person actually types, not the AI-punchy style of super-short "trying to be interesting" sentences. The punchy style reads as AI and people can tell it's fake. Benchmark = a real story told in normal sentences, with a small honest admission, landing on a natural insight. [CALIBRATE: pull benchmark posts from Mauro's own transcripts and published posts as they accumulate.]

**Why:** Accounts that perform look real and natural, like something a person actually wrote. What drives results is the only test that matters, and creative-to-stop-people staccato has not been it.

**How to apply:** Story-led, conversational, full sentences over staccato fragments. Stop forcing numbers-led punchy hooks on opinion/education posts. EXCEPTION: auto-DM posts still use the punchy format (confirmed those have to be that way). This rule is for normal posts only. Casing: standard capitalization (sentence case) is the house style. Natural voice does NOT mean lowercase. ALL-CAPS only for the occasional emphasis word.

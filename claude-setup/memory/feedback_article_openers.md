Do not open every Lorenzo article with the spend/credential badge ("$107M in managed spend"). Mauro flagged on 2026-06-04 that nearly every article leads with this and it has become a tic that trains the reader to skip the first 4 lines. The opener should be decided by the *input*: read the source material, find the single strongest, most specific hook that piece gives you, and lead with that. Options include a source/news hook (the thread or post the piece is built on), a contradiction/counterintuitive claim, an operational stat, or the spend anchor. Credibility does not have to live at the top; it often lands harder lower, attached to a specific claim (a named client result like Blaze $50k→$1M usually beats a spend badge in line 1).

**Why:** Repetition of the same opener kills the hook and signals templated/AI content; the spend number stops earning attention when it shows up every time.

**How to apply:** When running [[project_lorenzo_x_account]] content or the x-article-creator skill, pick the opener type that fits the input. The skill (skills/content/x-article-creator.md, v1.4) was updated to make the opener input-driven. Relates to [[feedback_writing_style]] and [[feedback_andromeda_visual_difference]].

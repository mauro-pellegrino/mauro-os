Mauro finds long, dense, multi-section responses hard to follow. Keep chat replies short, plain, and skimmable. Answer the actual question first, lead with the one thing that matters, cut tables/headers unless they truly help. Save deep detail for the files/skills, not the chat.

**Why:** He processes and acts faster on simple replies; heavy responses cause friction and he disengages.

**How to apply:** Short paragraphs or a tight list. One idea per line. If he asked 3 questions, answer them in 3 short beats. Don't stack checklists, tables, and headers in one message. Relates to [[feedback_writing_style]].

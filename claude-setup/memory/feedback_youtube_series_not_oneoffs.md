YouTube video ideas must be pitched as episodes of a repeatable series, not one-off videos.

**Why:** One-off videos force a cold start on every upload (new concept, new format, new packaging). A series compounds: the format gets faster to produce each episode, viewers know what they subscribed for, and the back catalog cross-sells itself.

**How to apply:** When generating YouTube ideas, define the series first (name, repeatable format, who it serves), then pitch individual videos as episodes of it. If an idea can't sustain at least 5 episodes, it's a segment inside an existing series, not a new video.

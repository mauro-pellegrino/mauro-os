Never save scripts, articles, or any generated content to the repo until Mauro has read and approved them in the conversation.

**Why:** Mauro almost always needs to correct content before it's usable. Saving drafts prematurely creates noise and extra work cleaning up the repo.

**How to apply:** Always paste content in chat first. Wait for explicit approval or corrections. Only then save and push. This applies to everything in `scripts/`, `research/`, and any other content files.

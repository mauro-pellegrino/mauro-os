## The thumbnail formula

- **His face in 9 of 10.** Direct-to-camera or profile, serious / intense / somber. He almost never smiles.
- **Two archetypes, and he alternates:**
  - **Cinematic photo + one loaded prop** that IS the promise: cash stacks (wealth), empty mansion (hollow wealth), EEG cap + phone ("the science of your brain"), tally wall (discipline), tier-list rail (authority ranking).
  - **Hand-drawn whiteboard / Miro board + facecam** for the framework videos. The thumbnail is the diagram.
- **Almost no typed text.** The image carries it; the title does the words. When text appears it's handwritten (a sign) or part of the diagram.
- **High subject/background contrast**, moody-dark or clean-bright, filmic color grade.
- **He reuses winners** (the brain-cap/phone motif shows up twice, #1 and #10).

## The title formula

- **Absolutes + curiosity:** "You'll NEVER [X] again after watching this" (used twice). NEVER/ALWAYS + AGAIN + "after watching this" curiosity gap.
- **Fake-precise stats:** "99.6% of men," "50,000 men," "$40M," "10x." Oddly specific numbers read as real.
- **Time boxes and constraints:** "Give me 13 mins," "12 hours a day," "10 years in 1 year," "while working 9-5."
- **Identity anchors:** "millionaire CEO," "men," "millionaire in 2026."
- **Save-you-effort authority:** "I Ranked Every X So You Don't Have To."
- **Hypothetical rebuild:** "Step by step how I'd make $1,000,000…"
- **Universal callout:** "You All Have The Same Problem."
- **Topic:** broad money, discipline, dopamine, mindset. Not niche agency. This is the lever for subs.

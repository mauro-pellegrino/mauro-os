## Core insights (for the magnet hook + bullets)

1. **The real AI tell is sameness, not quality.** Everyone uses the same tools, so everyone sounds the same, and sameness is invisible. Attention goes to what's different.
2. **AI slop signals a slop service.** A prospect who sees an obviously-AI post assumes the work behind your actual service is just as lazy.
3. **The fix is a voice document, not less AI.** Built from real source material (how you talk, voice notes), refined with a lot of back and forth, plus hard rules and bans (no em dashes). Tell the AI to make you sound less like everyone else.
4. **Write like you speak.** Record yourself, type without AI for a while, then let the anti-AI voice be built from that.
5. **Beginner vs operator: one chat vs formats → projects → skills.** Dividing by format means each one gets its own voice, its own steps, and perfect context, so it compounds instead of resetting daily.
6. **Proof asset: the HTML-to-PNG.** Inspired by Wiz of VC, rebuilt in his own colors with Claude, exported as PNG to share on socials. ~1 day, lots of back and forth.
7. **The one change:** stop freezing in AI-anxiety. Record/type yourself, build the voice, add your sauce (comparisons), and just ship.

**Strongest single claim (hook candidate):** Everyone using AI sounds the same, and sameness is why no one pays attention to you. The cost is bigger than boredom: AI slop tells prospects your service is slop too.
